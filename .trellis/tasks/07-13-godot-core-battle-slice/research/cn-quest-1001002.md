## HP calculation

The converter ports `EnemySourceBase` hit-based HP:

```text
floor(375 * party_atk_multiplier * basic_multiplier * hit_count
      * row_correction * curve_correction * quest_correction)
```

Threshold selection is the first master key greater than or equal to enemy level, matching `GeneralEnemySourceHelper.getSurjectivity()`.

- Level-12 zako `slango`: 148 HP.
- Level-12 general boss `slango`: 13009 HP.

Curve rows come from the 1.8.1 APK bundled `*_iosbundled.orderedmap` tables. Quest/enemy rows come from the CN 1.4.54 resource set used by the CN client data flow.

## Slango state machine and funnel entities

The converter now follows the complete unconditional `slango` difficulty-1 cycle from `neutral1` back to `neutral1` across 36 states. It preserves state IDs, animation/marker sequence names, next-state links, and termination types. Canonical fire-state bindings are:

- `funnel_fire1` / `funnel_fire2` → funnel Action DSL.
- `shot1_fire1` through `shot1_fire4` → normal three-way shot.
- `skill1_fire1` → five-ring skill shot.

Time and animation-loop termination values are retained. The three movement states target `p1`, `p2`, and `p3`; because the verified terrain marker coordinates remain unavailable, only their durations use an explicitly marked 90-frame adapter.

The spawned level-15 `slango` funnel now has curve-derived 132 HP / 23 ATK evidence, a deterministic orbiting entity, and a 120-frame first-shot / 180-frame repeat adapter using the canonical zako shot. Damageable multi-body funnel collision remains part of the later full terrain/entity port.


## Terrain runtime adapter

The verified terrain AMF3 remains unavailable, so exact geometry is still not claimed. The packaged fixture now owns a typed `terrain_runtime` boundary with explicit `status: fallback`, deterministic collision `segments[]`, and named boss movement `markers` (`p1`, `p2`, `p3`). `FixedStepWorld` consumes the segments using deterministic circle-to-line closest-point projection, penetration correction, and approaching-only reflection with normal-impact reporting. Presentation renders the fallback segments for inspection. These coordinates must be replaced, not silently merged, if the canonical AMF3/TMX terrain is recovered.
## Damageable funnel entity

The level-15 `slango` funnel is now a real fixed-step collision entity rather than a render-only orbit record. It owns canonical 132 HP / 23 ATK, a stable serial-tagged body, independent impact damage, projectile scheduling, and deterministic owner-relative orbit updates. Destroying a funnel removes its body immediately, does not damage the boss, does not advance the zone objective, and leaves no ghost collider. Boss/zone teardown removes every remaining funnel body. Presentation receives body-free snapshots so simulation references do not leak across the presentation boundary.

## Skill event timing and conditions

The converter now recursively accumulates nested Action DSL `Event.Wait` frames instead of flattening every command to frame zero. For the checked party this preserves hit delays of 0, 3, and 80 frames. `CreateCondition` records are normalized without discarding their raw source payloads: Flying 480 frames, party AttackPoint +0.8 for 960 frames, and enemy Poison for 2400 frames with raw strength 1500 and interval 1.

Godot executes these records through a fixed-step pending-event queue. Conditions have explicit lifetimes, enemy conditions are cleared with the owning enemy, and delayed hits cannot fire early. Poison's current 60-frame tick mapping is still an adapter because the original interval unit has not yet been proven; the raw values remain packaged so this mapping can be replaced without reconverting content.

## Extended ability execution

The battle now tracks direct-attack count and fever points. DamageCount skill modifiers compare against the recovered threshold; paralysis/frozen slayers require the corresponding enemy condition; SkillMax additional direct attack requires the owning slot to be full. Fixed heal clamps to party maximum HP, and fever gain clamps to its runtime meter.

The original ability gear dispatch remains incomplete. In particular, the current mapping of `FixedHeal` and trigger-None `AddFeverPoint` to owner skill activation is a documented adapter derived from their skill-oriented statue groups, not yet a claim of complete gear-level parity.
